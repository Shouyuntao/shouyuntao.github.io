Biography

My research philosophy is to promote the development of customized multi-modal emotion recognition, and graph neural network, rather than the follower of others. My current research interests span computer vision, graph representation learning, multimodal emotion recognition and machine learning with a series of topics, such as frequency domain graph representation learning theory, graph contrastive learning, and remote sensing image processing.
